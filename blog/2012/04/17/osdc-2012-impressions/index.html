    OSDC.TW 2012: Summary & Impressions

    Last weekend I joined OSDC.TW 2012 (Open Source Developers’ Conference) in Sinica Academia, Taipei. It was a 2-day event (April 14-15, 2012) with 7 keynote speeches, 9 sessions (departed to 3 mini-confs for each, 27 topics in total), and 9 lightning talks. Here is the official agenda.
